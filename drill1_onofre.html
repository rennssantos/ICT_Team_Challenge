Volleyball

What is Volleyball?

a team sport where two teams, separated by a high net, use their hands to hit an inflated ball back and forth.

Key Elements
  • Passing (The Bump)
  • Setting
  • Spiking (Attacking)
  • Blocking
  • Digging
  • Serving
Positions
  • Setter
  • Outside Hitter
  • Opposite Hitter
  • Middle Blocker
