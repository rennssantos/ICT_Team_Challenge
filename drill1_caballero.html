What is VolleyBall

Volleyball is a fast-paced team sport where two teams of six players attempt to score points by grounding the ball on the opponent's side of the net. Teams use a maximum of three consecutive contacts (usually a bump, set, and spike) to return the ball, while preventing it from touching their own floor.

Rules

Rally Scoring: Points are scored on every single rally, regardless of who serves. The winning team of the rally gets a point and the next serve.
